# Project Lens 🔍

> A clearer lens on your research landscape.

An AI-powered platform that helps students across all academic fields
discover, plan, and develop their thesis, capstone, or research projects
— from idea generation to completion.

## The Problem

Students struggle with:
- Coming up with original, novel project ideas
- Finding relevant research papers and datasets
- Knowing if their idea already exists
- Identifying gaps they can uniquely fill
- Understanding what methods or tools to use

## The Solution

Project Lens is an intelligent platform that:
- **Generates** personalised project ideas based on your field and skills
- **Searches** academic papers (arXiv, Semantic Scholar, PubMed)
- **Checks novelty** — tells you how original your idea is
- **Finds datasets** from Kaggle, Hugging Face, and UCI
- **Analyses GitHub repos** for technology gaps and trends
- **Works for ANY field** — not just Computer Science
